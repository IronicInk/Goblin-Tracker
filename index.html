How It Works

Track your token strategy each turn:

Ready — Tokens that can attack or tap this turn
Sick — Summoning sickness. Just entered play, can't act yet
Tap Commander — Creates new tokens based on your commander's ability
New Turn — Moves all Sick tokens to Ready so they can fight
New Game — Resets this commander's board and turn counter. Other commanders' games are saved and unaffected
Clear Board — Wipes all tokens mid-game but keeps the turn counter running. Found in the menu (⋯)
